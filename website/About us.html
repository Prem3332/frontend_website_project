About Us

Metal Infrastructure | Roofing Solutions | Conveyor Systems | Custom Fabrications

What We Are

At Parmar Industries, we pride ourselves on being highly experienced and precise workers in the field of metal infrastructure. Since our establishment in 1970, we have specialized in providing top-quality metal roofing and shades solutions for both households and factories. Our expertise extends to the design and manufacturing of wheel conveyors, ensuring efficient material handling in various industrial applications. Additionally, we offer custom fabrication services, creating heavy-duty gates for airports and durable railings for stairs, among other tailored solutions. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail have made us a trusted partner in the industry, dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of our clients.

Our Mission

At Parmar Industries, our mission is to deliver exceptional metal infrastructure solutions with precision and expertise. Since 1970, we have been committed to enhancing the operational efficiency and safety of our clients through high-quality metal roofing, custom fabrication, and innovative conveyor systems. We strive to uphold the highest standards of craftsmanship and service, ensuring that each project is tailored to meet the unique needs of our customers. Our dedication to sustainability and continuous improvement drives us to explore new technologies and practices that benefit our clients, employees, and the environment. We aim to be the trusted partner of choice in the metal infrastructure industry, providing reliable solutions that stand the test of time.
